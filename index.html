About me

Hello! I'm a PhD student in computer science and statistics at Università Bocconi. My main interests include deep learning (theoretical, methodological, geometric), statistical physics and network analysis. I am also a member of the Free Software Foundation (and of the Church of Emacs).

I love physics, machine learning and writing free software.


This is my personal website, it contains general info about myself, my research and other topics that I find interesting.

This website is also fully static (HTML+CSS only), the code is available on GitHub.

Feel free to reach out to my email (above, in anti-scraping format) for anything!
